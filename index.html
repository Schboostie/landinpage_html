About Me

I am 34 years old and I work in South London helping homeless people find places to live.

I am passionate about coding. I studied mathematical modelling with the Open University after never completing school and I saw how intricately designed the world truly is. I wanted to code to express ideas share information and inspire others to learn.

I have been practising the basics with M1M0 and Grasshopper and I feel that the challenge is not only rewarding intellectually but emotionally as well.

My plans are to learn web development, build more data-science skills, apply what I have learnt to the exciting work of crypto and finally teach others and help them to follow their dreams

I am excited to starting this journey towards coding my future!
